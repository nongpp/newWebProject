General information page


Bachelor of Engineering Program Automotive engineering (English Program) is a 4-year course of 146 credits. About 75% content is like mechanical engineering.
The rest is a specialized course in automotive engineering that covers theoretical practical aspects in design. And production Have a project or cooperative education in the year of the end.
Teaching English By year 1-2 students will take basic courses at Rangsit Center and move to Pattaya Center for the 3rd - 4th year.


Costs and Scholarships

Expenses

This project is a special project. Tuition fee is 2,500 baht per semester, approximately 75,000 baht per semester. Total tuition fee for 4 years is about 600,000 baht. scholarship Students participating in this program will be given the opportunity to receive scholarships from SIAM GLOBAL COMPANY LIMITED, which will total 12 scholarships. Students are required to study at the second or higher level and must have a grade point average of more than 2.5.
